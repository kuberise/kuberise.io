A commercial license is required when you use kuberise.io to generate revenue or provide commercial services **and** you do not wish to comply with the AGPL-3.0 obligations. Typical scenarios include:

- **Managed service providers** -- Offering kuberise.io (or a modified version) as a hosted or managed service to third-party customers.

- **Proprietary products** -- Embedding or integrating kuberise.io into a proprietary product or platform that you distribute or operate commercially.
- **SaaS offerings** -- Running kuberise.io as part of a software-as-a-service product without making the source code of your modifications available to users.

## When is a commercial license NOT required?

You do **not** need a commercial license if:

- You use kuberise.io for **personal or educational** purposes.
- You use kuberise.io **internally** within your organisation for your own infrastructure (not as a service sold to others).
- You fully comply with the **AGPL-3.0** terms, including making the source code of any modifications available to users who interact with the software over a network.
- You are contributing to the **open-source project** itself.
